Domestic Violence







What is Domestic Violence?

Domestic violence is violence or other abuse that takes place in a domestic setting, such as in a marriage or cohabitation.
Domestic violence can take different forms, they are as follows:
physical abuse, which is the most common type of abuse consists of physical damage being impose on someone.
Emotional abuse, it involves mainly verbal which includes insults, verbal threat, gaslighting and many more.

Types of Domestic Abuse
  • Physical Abuse
    • Physical abuse is the most common type of abuse,
      consisting of physical damage being imposed on someone.

  • Emotional abuse
    • Emotional abuse involves mainly verbal which includes insults, verbal threats,
      gaslighting and many others.


  • Sexual Violence
    • This type of violence is considered as a serious public health problem and has a profound
      short or long-term impact on physical and mental health.
      It involves crimes like sexual assault, rape and sexual abuse.


  • Psychological Violence
    • Psychological Violence also known as mental abuse includes
      acts of threatening, harassment,
      verbal aggression, and many more.
